About Us

About

The Northumbrian Mechanic is your local, trusted electrician serving Berwick-upon-Tweed, the Scottish borders, and the surrounding areas of Northumberland. With years of experience in the industry, we offer a wide range of mechanical services, including servicing, repairs, brakes, welding, exhausts, MOT preparation/failure correction, and more.

We pride ourselves on providing high-quality, reliable work at competitive prices. Our commitment to customer satisfaction means we always go the extra mile to ensure you're happy with the results. No matter what your car needs, from a simple oil change to a complete engine rebuild, you can count on us for expert workmanship and exceptional service.
